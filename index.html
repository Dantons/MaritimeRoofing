Local Crews, Local Knowledge

Atlantic Canada roofing is not like roofing anywhere else. Salt air corrodes cheap fasteners. Nor'easters drive rain horizontally under flashing that would hold fine in Ontario. Freeze-thaw cycles crack rigid sealants that work perfectly in milder climates. We have been roofing in this environment for two decades, and every material choice, installation technique, and ventilation design we use is specifically selected for Maritime conditions.

Our crews live in the communities we serve. When we say we will be there at 8 AM, we mean it — because we are driving from down the road, not from a head office in Toronto. That local presence means faster emergency response, better knowledge of municipal permit requirements, and accountability you can count on. If something goes wrong with your roof, you know where to find us.
